A Carpet and Flooring SEO Success Story

Client Overview: Carpet and Flooring Services Agency in USA

Carpet and Flooring Services Agency in the USA specializes in transforming homes and businesses with expert carpet cleaning, flooring installation, repair, stretching, and odor removal services. Operating primarily in Georgia locales such as Dacula, Snellville, Duluth, Buford, Loganville, and Lawrenceville, the agency faced challenges in a competitive local market where online discoverability was key to attracting clients. Before our partnership, their digital footprint was limited, with many service-related searches yielding poor results or no visibility at all.

Initial Challenges and Opportunities

Upon engagement, a thorough audit highlighted several barriers to growth:

  • Visibility Gaps: Core keywords like "professional carpet cleaning Dacula" and "luxury vinyl plank installation Snellville" were either buried beyond the top 10 or not ranking in the top 200 as of May 2025.

  • Traffic and Engagement Issues: Website analytics showed inconsistent organic traffic, with low user acquisition from search engines.

  • Local Profile Underutilization: The Google Business Profile (GBP) received minimal interactions, views, and conversions, resulting in missed local map pack opportunities.

  • Content and Technical Shortfalls: Sparse location-specific content, unoptimized pages, and a lack of authoritative backlinks contributed to stagnant rankings..

    Strategic Overhaul: Our Customized Plan

    From May to December 2025, we deployed a multi-faceted SEO initiative tailored to the agency's local focus:

  • Targeted Keyword Optimization: Researched and integrated 35+ keywords, emphasizing geo-specific terms to capture high-intent searches.

  • Content and On-Page Enhancements: Created detailed service pages and blogs addressing common queries, such as pet odor removal and tile cleaning, while fixing technical issues like site speed and mobile responsiveness.

  • Backlink Building Campaign: Secured 187 quality backlinks through articles (4), blogs (6), press releases (2), guest posts (4), submissions (20), classified ads (20), community participation (20), business profiles (20), GBP links (40), social media posts (30), videos (1), and GBP posts (20).

  • Local SEO Boost:Revamped the GBP with regular updates, review encouragement, and optimized listings to improve map visibility.

  • Performance Tracking: Monitored via Google Analytics and GBP insights, iterating based on data for sustained momentum.

    • This approach aimed at not just rankings but tangible business outcomes like calls and website visits.

    Measurable Wins: From Obscurity to Dominance

    The efforts yielded clear, data-backed progress by December 12, 2025:

  • Ranking Surge: 86% of tracked keywords improved, with 51% entering the top 10. Average positions climbed significantly, driving more organic exposure. Standouts include:

  • "Professional carpet cleaning Dacula": Climbed from #11 (May) to #2 (Dec).

  • Upholstery cleaning Duluth GA": From not in top 200 (May) to #6 (Dec).

  • New additions like "Carpet repair services in Snellville": From not in top 200 to #3 (Dec).

  • Conversion Uplift: Organic channels delivered 6 calls and 6 form submissions in the same period, signaling stronger lead generation.

  • GBP Performance: November saw 123 interactions and 2,280 views, translating to 49 calls (+28.9% vs. Nov 2024) and 74 website clicks, direct boosts to business inquiries.

    Lessons Learned and Path Forward

    Key takeaways include the power of localized content and consistent backlinking in competitive niches. Moving ahead, we recommend ongoing content refreshes, seasonal campaigns (e.g., holiday cleaning tips), and advanced analytics integration to sustain momentum.

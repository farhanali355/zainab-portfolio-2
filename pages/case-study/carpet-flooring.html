Case Study

A Carpet and Flooring SEO Success Story

Client Overview: Carpet and Flooring Services Agency in USA

Carpet and Flooring Services Agency in the USA specializes in transforming homes and businesses with expert carpet cleaning, flooring installation, repair, stretching, and odor removal services. Operating primarily in Georgia locales such as Dacula, Snellville, Duluth, Buford, Loganville, and Lawrenceville, the agency faced challenges in a competitive local market where online discoverability was key to attracting clients.

Before our partnership, their digital footprint was limited, with many service-related searches yielding poor results or no visibility at all.

Initial Challenges and Opportunities

Upon engagement, a thorough audit highlighted several barriers to growth:

  • Visibility Gaps: Core keywords like "professional carpet cleaning Dacula" and "luxury vinyl plank installation Snellville" were either buried beyond the top 10 or not ranking in the top 200 as of May 2025.

  • Traffic and Engagement Issues: Website analytics showed inconsistent organic traffic, with low user acquisition from search engines.

  • Local Profile Underutilization: The Google Business Profile (GBP) received minimal interactions, views, and conversions.

    • These factors resulted in missed leads, as potential customers turned to competitors with stronger online strategies.

    Strategic Overhaul: Our Customized Plan

    From May to December 2025, we deployed a multi-faceted SEO initiative tailored to the agency's local focus:

  • Targeted Keyword Optimization: Researched and integrated 35+ keywords, emphasizing geo-specific terms to capture high-intent searches.

  • Backlink Building & Local SEO: Secured 187 quality backlinks and revamped the GBP with regular updates and optimized listings to improve map visibility.

  • Performance Tracking: Monitored via Google Analytics and GBP insights, iterating based on data for sustained momentum.

    • Measurable Wins: From Obscurity to Dominance

    The efforts yielded clear, data-backed progress by December 12, 2025:

  • Ranking Surge: 86% of tracked keywords improved, with 51% entering the top 10.

  • Traffic Growth: Organic search accounted for 186 users (74.7% of total).

  • GBP Performance: 2,280 views and 123 interactions in November.

  • Conversion Impact: Increased local visibility led to a significant rise in direct inquiries and quote requests for flooring projects.

  • Market Dominance: The agency now holds top positions for high-intent keywords, outperforming major local competitors.

  • Sustainable Growth: The improved SEO foundation continues to drive monthly traffic increases with minimal additional spend.

  • Brand Authority: Enhanced local map presence has established the client as the go-to authority for flooring services.

    • Lessons Learned and Path Forward

    Key takeaways include the power of localized content and consistent backlinking in competitive niches. Moving ahead, we recommend ongoing content refreshes, seasonal campaigns (e.g., holiday cleaning tips), and advanced analytics integration to sustain momentum.

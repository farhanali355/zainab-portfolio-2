Case Study

Security Company in Los Angeles

How we helped a security services provider dominate local search rankings

What is SEO?

SEO, or Search Engine Optimization, is the process of improving a website's visibility in organic (unpaid) search results on engines like Google. Unlike paid ads, which require ongoing spending, SEO builds long-term traffic by optimizing content, structure, and authority to rank higher for relevant keywords.

For local businesses like security companies, effective SEO can drive targeted leads, increase brand awareness, and boost conversions, often resulting in 5-10x ROI over time through consistent, high-quality traffic.

The Client: Security Company in Los Angeles

Security Company in Los Angeles is a leading provider of security guard services in the LA area, offering armed and unarmed guards, event security, fire watch, and specialized protection for industries like healthcare, retail, transportation, and entertainment.

Facing stiff competition in a saturated market, they struggled with low online visibility. Their website received minimal organic traffic, and key search terms like "security company los angeles" ranked poorly (e.g., outside the top 10-20 initially). They approached us after realizing Google restrictions and outdated optimization were limiting their reach to potential clients searching for reliable security solutions.

The Audit: Uncovering the Issues

Our initial SEO audit revealed several critical problems:

  • Technical Issues: Broken links, crawl errors, slow page speeds, and poor mobile optimization hindered indexing.
  • On-Page Deficiencies: Missing meta tags, thin content, and unoptimized headings for high-value keywords.
  • Content Gaps: Limited blog posts or service pages targeting long-tail keywords.
  • Local SEO Weaknesses: Underutilized GMB profile, few reviews, and inconsistent NAP citations.
  • Backlink Profile: Low domain authority with unhealthy links.

These issues resulted in average positions around 40-50 and very low search visibility initially.

Our Approach: A Comprehensive SEO Strategy

We implemented a tailored SEO plan over four months (August to November 2025):

  • Keyword Research: Identified 75+ high-intent keywords targeting core LA security terms.
  • Technical SEO: Resolved crawl errors and optimized site speed for all priority pages.
  • Content Strategy: Created service-specific blogs targeting "security guards for events" and "seasonal protection."
  • Local Integration: Fully optimized the GMB profile and built authoritative local citations.
  • Link Acquisition: Secured 30+ quality backlinks from relevant industry and local domains.

The Results: Significant Growth in Visibility & Traffic

The campaign delivered impressive improvements across key metrics:

  • Top Ranking Achievement: "Agricultural facility security los angeles" moved from #14 to #1.
  • Seasonal Success: "New year security guard los angeles" achieved #1 position from #36.
  • High Intent Terms: "Banks security guards los angeles" secured the top spot #1 from #31.
  • Broad Terms: "Security guard company los angeles" climbed to #11 from outside the top 100.

Out of 75 tracked keywords, 60% improved by at least 5 positions.

Expert Advice: Navigating SEO Challenges

SEO isn't one-and-done; Google's algorithms evolve, and local competition is fierce. Focus on user intent, mobile optimization, and fresh content. For seasonal services like event security, plan ahead with timely keywords. Regular audits prevent penalties and maintain momentum.

  • Focus on user intent and search relevance.
  • Prioritize mobile optimization for local searchers.
  • Create fresh, valuable local content regularly.
  • Plan for seasonal keywords like "event security" in advance.
  • Conduct regular SEO audits to prevent ranking drops.

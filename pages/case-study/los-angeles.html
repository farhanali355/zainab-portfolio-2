The Client & The Challenge

The Client

Security Company in Los Angeles is a leading provider of armed and unarmed security services, facing fierce local competition and struggling with online visibility.

The Core Objective

Reverse the downward visibility trend and establish a dominant, lead-generating online presence in a highly competitive Los Angeles market.

The Challenge

Initial analysis revealed poor search visibility (rankings in the 40-50 range), technical site errors, content gaps, and an underutilized GMB profile, leading to low organic traffic.

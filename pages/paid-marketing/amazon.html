What is Amazon Ads Management?

Amazon Ads management is the professional management of advertising campaigns on Amazon's advertising platform, designed to help sellers, vendors, and brands increase product visibility, drive sales, and maximize ROI on the world's largest e-commerce marketplace.

Why Amazon Ads Management Matters

With over 300 million active customer accounts and 9.1 million sellers worldwide, Amazon is the dominant e-commerce platform. Amazon Ads reach customers at the precise moment they're ready to purchase, making it the most effective platform for e-commerce advertising.

Core Amazon Ads Components

Amazon Advertising offers multiple campaign types, each designed for specific objectives and targeting capabilities within the Amazon ecosystem.

Sponsored Products: Promote individual product listings
Sponsored Brands: Showcase your brand and multiple products
Sponsored Display: Reach shoppers on and off Amazon
Amazon DSP: Programmatic advertising at scale

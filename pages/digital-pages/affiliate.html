Miami Boat Rental:
Scaling Luxury Bookings

How data-driven PPC optimization transformed a stagnant ad account into a lead production machine.

01. Context

Client Background

Our client is a premier boat rental and yacht charter company in Miami, operating a diverse fleet of luxury vessels. Despite high demand, their existing PPC campaigns were underperforming, leading to wasted spend.

The Pain Point

Burning $500+/day on generic keywords with zero tracked phone calls.

The Initial Audit

  • High CPC due to broad match keywords.
  • Missing geographical bid modifiers.
  • No conversion tracking for phone calls.
02. Strategy

Data-Driven Optimization

We completely restructured the keyword hierarchy. By moving from broad search terms to "Long-Tail" and "Exact Match" keywords, we ensured maximum ROI.

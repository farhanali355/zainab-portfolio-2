What is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a performance-based marketing strategy where affiliates earn commissions by promoting other companies' products or services. It's a win-win partnership that generates revenue for both merchants and affiliates.

The Power of Affiliate Marketing

The affiliate marketing industry is projected to reach $15.7 billion by 2024. It offers low-risk, high-reward opportunities for individuals and businesses to generate passive income through strategic partnerships.

How Affiliate Marketing Works

Affiliate marketing operates on a simple commission model where affiliates earn money by driving sales, leads, or actions for merchant partners through their promotional efforts.

Affiliate joins merchant's program and gets unique tracking links
Affiliate promotes products through content, ads, or social media
Merchant pays commission for referred sales, leads, or clicks

Key Affiliate Marketing Models

Pay-Per-Sale (PPS)

Earn commissions only when your referral results in a completed sale. This model offers higher commission rates and focuses on direct revenue generation.

Direct Sales Revenue Share High Commissions Performance-Based

Pay-Per-Lead (PPL)

Earn commissions for generating qualified leads, such as newsletter sign-ups, form submissions, or free trial registrations for the merchant.

Lead Generation Form Submissions Trial Sign-ups Lower Risk

Pay-Per-Click (PPC)

Earn commissions based on the number of clicks your affiliate links generate, regardless of whether those clicks convert into sales.

Traffic Generation Click-Based Immediate Earnings Volume Focused

Recurring Commissions

Earn ongoing commissions for subscription-based products or services, creating sustainable passive income streams from a single referral.

Subscription Models Monthly Revenue Long-Term Earnings Passive Income
